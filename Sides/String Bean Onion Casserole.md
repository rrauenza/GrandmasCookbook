## String Bean Onion Casserole

* 350 Degrees
* 1 1/2 quart baking dish
* 30 min. plus 5 min.
* Serves: 4
* Cook Time: 30 min.

### Ingredients

* 2 - 14 1/2 oz. cans green beans - blue lake cut
* 1 1/3 cups French's Original Crispy Fried Onions
* 3/4 cup milk
* 1 - 10 1/2 can cream of mushroom soup
* 1/8 teaspoon French's Original Crispy Fried Onions

### Directions

Combine soup, milk and pepper in a 1 1/2 -quart baking dish; stir until blended. Stir in beans and 2/3 cup French Fried Onions. Bake at 350Â°F for 30 minutes or until hot. Stir. Sprinkle with remaining 2/3 cup onions. Bake 5 minutes or until onions are golden.
