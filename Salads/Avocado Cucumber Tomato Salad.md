# Salads

## Avocado Cucumber Tomato Salad

* Serves: 3

### Ingredients

* 2 avocados
* 1/4 cup cilantro
* 1  cucumber
* 1 medium lemon
* 1/2  red onion
* 1 Roma cilantro
* 1  zucchini
* 1/8 teaspoon  pepper
* 1 teaspoon  salt
* 2 tablespoons  olive oil

### Directions

Cut vegetables and herbs.  Add olive oil, salt, pepper and squeeze lemon.
