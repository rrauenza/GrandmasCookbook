# Sides

## Bev's New Salsa

* May 2020

### Ingredients

* 1 can  Fire Roasted Tomatoes
* 2 chopped jalapenos
* 4 -5 chopped green chili peppers
* 1/2 chopped onion
* to taste granulated garlic
* to taste pepper
* 1 bunch cilantro
* to taste ground cumin
* 1 chopped New Mexico chili
* to taste salt

### Directions

Combine all ingredients.  Sit overnight.
